What this proves (primary simulation evidence for Ch. 9):
  - the AXI4-Stream input (MM2S model) and output (S2MM model) datapath produces the correct
    token IDs (identical to HuggingFace bert-base-uncased),
  - m_axis_tlast is asserted on EXACTLY the final token of each response (DMA end-of-packet),
  - the AXI-Lite TOKEN_COUNT register (0x0C) reports the right token count (what firmware reads).

Cases cover the three word shapes: single-token, two-piece, and three-piece (continuation).
